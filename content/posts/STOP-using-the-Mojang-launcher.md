The Mojang launcher is very outdated compared to other launchers like Prism, ATLauncher, among others. The Mojang launcher lacks support for modpacks and is challenging to work with.

## Instances

Additionally, these launchers manage Minecraft files much more easily with the Instances system. This allows you to have different setups, for example, you can have one instance with Fabric, another with Forge, and even another with Quilt. And these won't have any conflicts because they work independently, and no instance interferes with another.

## Launchers

Here are two very good launchers for playing and developing modpacks.

- [Prism Launcher](https://prismlauncher.org/download/)
- [ATLauncher](https://atlauncher.com/downloads)
